
# The Flowers Edge (Blomsterkanten)

A 2D social chat game set in a magical botanical kingdom where anthropomorphic flowers, plants, and garden creatures live in harmony.

* Socialize and make new friends.
* Gather resources and trade.
* Play minigames with other players for livs.


## Setting

The game takes place in a futuristic botanical research facility nestled within an enormous biodome. This high-tech garden combines sleek sci-fi architecture with lush plant life, where genetically enhanced flowers and sentient garden creatures called "livs" thrive. Players explore different hangout spots like the greenhouse café, sunny meadows, and flower patches—while making friends and discovering the secrets of this peaceful plant-filled world.

## Resources

There are three main types of resources in The Flowers Edge.
* Petals
* Seeds
* Gold

**Petals**

Petals are gathered by nurturing personal enchanted gardens of various types of flowers.
Gardens will produce flowers at various intervals that depend on the type of flower planted. Types of flowers are:

1. Rose (Difficulty: Easy)
2. Tulip (Difficulty: Easy)
3. Daisy (Difficulty: Easy)
4. Pansy (Difficulty: Easy)
5. Zinnia (Difficulty: Easy)
6. Sunflower (Difficulty: Easy)
7. Orchid (Difficulty: Medium)
8. Lily (Difficulty: Medium)
9. Marigold (Difficulty: Medium)
10. Daffodil (Difficulty: Medium)
11. Hydrangea (Difficulty: Medium)
12. Peony (Difficulty: Medium)
13. Carnation (Difficulty: Medium)
14. Snapdragon (Difficulty: Medium)
15. Iris (Difficulty: Medium)
16. Aster (Difficulty: Hard)
17. Chrysanthemum (Difficulty: Hard)
18. Foxglove (Difficulty: Hard)
19. Bleeding Heart (Difficulty: Hard)
20. Calla Lily (Difficulty: Hard)
21. Fuchsia (Difficulty: Hard)
22. Gardenia (Difficulty: Hard)
23. Hibiscus (Difficulty: Hard)
24. Tulip (Difficulty: Hard)

Petals can be converted into either seeds or gold.

**Seeds**

Seeds allow for growing more flowers of the seeds type inside personal enchanted gardens. 
Seeds that the player already has can be planted and converted to petals, thereby giving the player more seeds of this type.
New seed types can be purchased with gold. 

**Gold**

Gold is the currency in the game. It can be used to purchase player cosmetics, seeds, enchanted garden etc.
It's also possible to coinflip other players for more gold.
